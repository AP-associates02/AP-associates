about us

the best Architecture and Structure Designer in the city

“AP Associates” having its registered office at near Maharishi school, Lakhperabagh Barabanki is a group of architects , Interior designers, structural engineers, project managers and other services consultants. The firm is equipped with latest infrastructure facilities and modest presentation techniques. The range of services vary from comprehensive architectural design, interior design, structural design, layout design, master planning , landscape design, estimation of project. The range of projects vary from Multi storied housing schemes, commercial complexes, hospitals, hotels, resorts and restaurants, recreational/ cultural centers, residential houses to exclusive villas/ farm houses. We lay great stress on economical design through innovative design ideas, material selection and specifications. At the same time due importance is being given to functional and aesthetic part. The policy of our organization is most effectively implemented by the management and senior associates who possess diverse qualification and broad spectrum of experience in their chosen field of endeavor. We take pride in our professional integrity and most complete co- operation and co-ordination between clients and ourselves. These qualities have had a tremendous impact on the design and functional viability of the projects executed by us. The organization with its dynamic approach and fastidiousness for accuracy and quality, coupled with swiftness in execution, has won all round acclaim.
